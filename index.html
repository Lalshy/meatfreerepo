The Challenge

Meat Free Week challenges participants to give up meat for seven days and raise funds for a great cause. Going meat free for one week creates a great opportunity to start thinking about how much meat you eat and the impact eating too much meat can have. Everyone is invited to take the Meat Free Week challenge and discover how easy it is to make little changes that can create a big difference. . Challenge yourself, your family, your friends and colleagues to give up meat for seven days.
